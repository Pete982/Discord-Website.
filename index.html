Hi!

Discord is a group-chatting platform originally built for gamers that has since become a general use platform for many kinds of communities. Discord also allows users to voice- and video-chat, as well as livestream games and other programs from their computers. This platform also allows and expects only people 13 and up to join and anyone under thirteen and not thirteen will be banned. This platform is available in 28 different languages and was made May 13 2015. This platform was originally used for people who play video games, but now is a platform for a lot more including socializing, meeting people, calling, chatting, sending images, talking and more.
